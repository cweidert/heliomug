Face Card / Kill Card

Face Card Kill Card is a game that my friends and I made up while we were playing poker.

Each card is worth the number shown on the card with the exception of aces, which are worth 11. Face cards are worth nothing. In addition to the face up cards, you have one hole card that only you know.

You want to get the highest total score without drawing a face card, which eliminates you from the game.
