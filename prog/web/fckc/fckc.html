Face Card / Kill Card

Face Card Kill Card is a game that my friends and I made up while we were playing poker.

The cards are worth the number shown on the card, with the exception aces, which are worth 11. Face cards are worth nothing. Basically, you want to get the highest total without drawing a face card, which eliminates you from the game.
